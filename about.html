About Our Farm

Today, modern dairy farms use advanced technologies to maximize milk production and improve the efficiency and quality of dairy products. Milk and milk products remain an important part of the human diet and are used in a wide variety of foods and products.Indian Cow and Buffalo Breeds: Indian cow breeds like Gir, Sahiwal, and khillar, as well as buffalo breeds like Murrah,jawary and pandharpuri,are known for producing A2 milk. Each breed may have its unique characteristics in terms of milk yield, fat content, and adaptability to local conditions. Milking Practices: Implementing proper milking practices is vital to maintain the quality of milk. Cleanliness of milking equipment, udder, and hands of the milker are crucial to prevent contamination. A2 Milk Certification: Some dairy farms go through certification processes to verify and label their milk as A2 milk. This may involve genetic testing of the cows and buffaloes to confirm the absence of the A1 protein. Product Diversification: Besides fresh A2 milk, consider diversifying our dairy product offerings. This may include producing A2 ghee, yogurt, and other value-added products.
